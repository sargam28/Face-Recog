Face reading using Face Recogniton

A facial recognition system is a technology capable of matching a human face from a digital image or a video frame against a database of faces. Many people are familiar with face recognition technology through the FaceID used to unlock iPhones (however, this is only one application of face recognition). Question now comes how can we read a face and tell about person's personality using this technology? Using Face recognition techniques , we can gather informtaion about a person's mood percentage , their appearance , and their facial structure . With additional information , we can gather all of these and perform an evaluation . And henceforth , we can know about someone's personality , their traits , their horoscope , and many other things.
